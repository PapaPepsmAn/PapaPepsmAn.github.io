Conclusion

The message conveyed in the film is that contemporary technologies allows people throughout the world to connect with each other without having to be in the same room, building, city or country. I have used many of the same pathways to highlight the message in the essay, through Google Search for research and information on the film, YouTube for imagery and video snippets, and word processing applications to write and format the information. Without modern media programs and editing highlighting and confirming my points relating to the question my audience would not have a connection to my digital essay.

Without contemporary technology Saroo Brierley would have never been able to reunite with his birth mother and the rest of his family. The remoteness of Saroo’s home village from the city and living thousands of kilometres away in Australia would have meant that this story would never existed. Garth Davies has managed to portray the distances and the problem facing Saroo, he shows the hours at the laptop that Saroo spends researching and staring at Google Earth. It is contemporary technology that solves everything when Saroo recognises pictures on a Google Earth application that he has memories of. The director has proven that “Contemporary technologies have the power to create a connection or disconnection” with his portrayal of the story line and the characters and use of modern film techniques.
