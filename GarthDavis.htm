Google Earth Applications

The use of Google Earth applications and its imagery throughout the film provides the connection between Saroo’s two worlds, zooming from Australia to a remote Indian village at the beginning of the film and while the adult Saroo commences his obsessive search for his birth family using his laptop and the world wide web from his lounge room. He doesn’t need to travel to India personally as all of the information he needs is at his fingertips and he begins his search from his childhood memories. Saroo utilises the advancements made in technology to make the connection, faster internet speeds, satellite imagery and cheap access to the internet for remote and poor areas. Without this access to information and visuals Saroo’s personal connection would be impossible.
