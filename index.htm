What is Lion?

Lion (2016) is the true story of Saroo Brierley, a man who when he was five years old was separated from his family and lost in the city of Calcutta. Saroo was adopted into an Australian family, but, never forgetting his birth family, he secretly searches for years on the internet looking for them. Without contemporary technologies his connection to his past would never have been made, being reunited with his mother would have remained a dream. The director, Garth Davis, has incorporated modern film techniques to make the film inclusive of the viewer, drawing them into the film as if they were beside Saroo on his journey.
